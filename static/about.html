What it is

NutriLog is a calorie and nutrient tracker.

I am developing this app to help a family member track their nutritional input.

How it works

NutriLog fetches calorie and nutrient information from the USDA FoodData Central database.

NutriLog uses an advanced machine learning algorithm returns the first match from the USDA database. As such, nutrient information may not be accurate. I'm working on improving the match.
